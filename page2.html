CLÁUDIO VIDAL // PT // b.1979

Multidisciplinary artist who that's being developing his work of artistic research, creation and training in the Butoh dance field since 2011.
The matrix of his work results from a tension between coexisting forces: decay, death, birth; the beautiful, the miracle and the horror. His research proposes a plunge into the unknown, a listening attentive to the madness of the present body, either to excite it, express it or let it express itself.

>> Co-artistic director of the group DEMO (Experimental Multidisciplinary and Organic Device)
>> Performer / musician in the musical project Lucifer's Ensemble.

BIO

Cláudio Vidal (PT) /// Creator, director, performer, butoh dancer, degree in Design and Communication from EUAC, (2010). Attended the ArtHumanNature course at the Nordic School of Butoh, taught by Anita Saij (2011).
He is co-founder and member of the DEMO performance company (2010). Since then, he has co-directed and interpreted artistic projects developed by DEMO, highlighting his participation as: co-director and performer in the Russian Roulette (2011) , at the Center for Contemporary Art Ujazdowski Castle (Warsaw); co-director and interpreter at Fios de Terra (2012), integrated in the European Capital of Culture - Guimarães 2012); co-director and interpreter at HYDRA & ORPHEU (2015), co-produced with the Jazz Ao Centro Club, funded by the Rectory of UC and supported by DRCN, presented at the Literary Festival The Rota das Letras Macau; participated in the International Colloquium Concepts and Devices of Creation in Performative Arts with the lecture-performance DISPOSITIVO EXPERIMENTAL, MULTIDISCIPLINAR E ORGÂNICO, organized by the CEIS20 of the UC, Center of Contemporary Dramatics and TAGV; co-director and interpreter at HIATUS (2017) with the support of TAGV, CCVF, gnration; co-director and interpreter at ARMADILHA (2019) in co-production with CCVF / Centro Cultural Vila Flor with a debut at FITEI / International Festival of Expression Iberian Theater 2019.

Projects

DEMO

DEMO is a device for artistic research, creation and production grounded in the visual and performative arts, while its practical purpose is to put together performances both in Theatre and Performance Art, and Music, to organize workshops, exhibitions, publications and other socio-cultural activities.

www.demo.pt

LUCIFER'S ENSEMBLE

LUCIFER'S ENSEMBLE is a music performance project crossing physical theatre, interactive technologies and sound art. An inverted triangle comprising two performers and a musician/composer.

www.lucifersensemble.org
